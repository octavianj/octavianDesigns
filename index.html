about

What's up? If you've made it this far, you for checking out my website! If not, well I guess you won't see this now will you? Anyways, my name is Octavian Johnson and I am a *inhales* web designer, graphic designer, programmer, illustrator, animator, photographer, videographer and halfway decent roller skater. Follow me on social media and tell me what anime I should watch next!

I made this about page pretty long so I guess I'll tell you a bit more about myself. I was born in Sumter, South Carolina in '91 and I'm currently living in Charleston, South Carolina. I've never been one to shy away from learning new things and I have no problem taking the time to do so (hence the roller skating thing). I have no doubt that I can help you with your projects.

Thanks to my dad, I've been drawing since I was a kid. I've been doing graphic design for about 10 years now and I am currently transitiong into the programming/web development space. I love taking photos and I'm actually hoping to upgrade my camera soon.

I've always been an overachiever and a bit of a perfectionist, which shows in my work. If there's something I don't know, I take the time to learn how to do it to the best of my ability to give the best output possible.
