black history playing cards

This is probably the project that I am the most proud of. With this project I created a full deck of playing cards that consists of many pivotal figures in black history. The deck is separated into four suits: sports, scients, music and activism. This project took me a few months to complete. With the color pallete, I chose red (which represents the struggle that black people have gone through) and purple (which represents royalty). I chose people that made big impacts in what they do/did, furthering the African American footprint in the United States. Each card has a blurb on it explaining these impacts. I created all of the figures in illustrator so that there would be a consistent style for all of them throughout. I then put them all together into the deck.
