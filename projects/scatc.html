south carolina aeronautical training center art project

During my time at Trident Tech, I was asked to draw an art piece that would be used in their Aeronautical Training Center. It's the newest building on the campus and they wanted some art to help add a bit of life to the already beautiful building. My piece consisted of a plane flying high as well as some of the robot arms used in the labs. I hope that this piece is able to brighten the halls of the building for years to come.
