space

I was tasked in this project with creating the branding for The Grind, a (fictional) coffee shop. The brief for the project said that they didn’t want to use browns like most coffee shops do, but they did want to keep it in the vein of using earth tones. I stayed in line with their requests by implementing a warm orange color along with a dark green to complement it. I created a logo that has the coffee cup pouring the last part of the “d” as if it’s “finishing” the logo. I brought it together with the complementing green color to resemble a plant. With this branding I created a style guide, a business card, a shirt design, an ad, a menu that would be used in the coffee shop, and coffee cup designs.
