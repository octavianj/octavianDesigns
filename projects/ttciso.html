trident tech iso high school campaign

At Trident Tech I was the main designer that worked on the yearly high school campaign. For 2022, we did an In Search Of (ISO) campaign which played off the idea of people searching for guidance on social media. I did the animation and design for the project while our videographer did the music and sfx.
