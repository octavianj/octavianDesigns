norse mythology book covers

This was a really fun project for me. Any project that makes me step out of my confort zone with different styles is a welcome one. With this project I created two book covers for Neil Gaiman’s Norse Mythology book. One style was created in an Art Nouveau style while the other was created in a Constructivism style. The first cover, done in the Art Nouveau style, is of a Valkyrie. Valkyries in Norse Mythology were a host of female figures who choose those who may die in battle and those who may live. Those who died were taken to Valhalla, the afterlife of Norse Mythology that was ruled by the god Odin. The other cover, done in the constructivist style, is of Mjolnir, Thor’s famous hammer. In Norse Mythology, Mjolnir was depicted as one of the most fearsome and powerful weapons in existence, capable of leveling mountains.

I chose to do the valkyrie in the Art Nouveau style because I felt that that their hair and armor would benefit from the linework that would be required to meet the qualifications of the style. I also felt that the power of Mjolnir could be accomplished with the bold yet simple style of constructivism. I tied the covers together with a similar color pallete that consisted of green tones complemented by a warm orange. I would really enjoy to do something like this again.
