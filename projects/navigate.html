trident tech navigate branding

During my time at Trident Tech, I created the branding for what is called The Hub, a place where new students can go to get help to prevent them from being lost or confused during their college career. The tagline is “Navigate Your Path to Success,” so with that I used directional navigation arrows in the wording. The arrows are similar to what you’d see on a compass. I used this in the wording to replace the “V” and the “A” in “Navigate” along with the dotted line pathways to go along with the navigate motif. Here is a postcard, a flyer for a mobile app, and signage that is used through the building. This signage includes window decals, navigation signs, as well as guiding signage inside of the building (not pictured).
