ghost & lamar childrens book

What better subjects for a children’s book than two crazy kids that I know. My children’s book consists of my dog Ghost and my son Lamar. I wanted to give off the idea that they’re both very energetic and are always getting into something. I hoped to create Calvin and Hobbes vibes with the designs of it. I was also heavily inspired by the look of Disney and Pixar characters and how animated and energetic they look, even when in still frame of their movies. I had a lot of fun with this project and it has inspired me to some day create more children’s books.
