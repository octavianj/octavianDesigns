illustration

Yin Yang

This illustration is one that had been sitting in my brain for months. I had done the sketch sometime last year (2021) and then I got deep into the programming space to prepare myself for my new job. I took a mental break between my studying to crank this out. At the time of writing this, we're in the first week of June and it's the only thing I've drawn so far this year. I had a ton of fun with it though!

In my journey from the past few years of trying to better my mental health. I tend to gravitate toward concepts and symbols that relate to the ups and downs of life. You can't have up without down, left without right, light without dark. You can't have positive without negative. The dualities of life are what gives it it's meaning. This is my own interpretation of that. A lot of my illustrations boil down to "just adding details (and flowers) so that they look cool," which is still true in this case but they're usually based on concepts or ideas that I think really relate to living a better life.
