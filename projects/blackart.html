black art magazine

I am really big on black representation in art and media so I created a mockup magazine called Black Art that would promote African American artists and creatives. With this particular issue I made myself the main artist of the month and I placed a few of my illustrations in it. I wrote an article about myself, designed the layout of the spread and placed an ad inside. The article is about my past struggles with depression and how I was able to get through it with art (and therapy). With the cover, I wanted to choose one of my most vibrant illustrations that would attract the most attention if the magazine had been on a shelf somewhere. In all, I designed the cover, the inside spread and I placed an ad from my Black Minds Matter campaign.
