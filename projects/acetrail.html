ace trail posters

These posters were made to market a new trail in Colleton County, South Carolina called the Ace Trail. I was tasked with illustrating 3 posters that would be used to promote the trail and to encourage people to donate to the funding of it. The first two posters play off of the idea of the ace card in a typical playing card deck and asks donors to “Bet on the Ace.” I tied these together with the playing card imagery in them both with the card on the bicycle and the entire second poster being a playing card. The third poster is a more general poster that would be for more widely used marketing. I brought all three posters together with a similar illustration style and texture. This was a really fun project, especially figuring out the perspective of the bicycle, which consisted of me searching for a 3D model of a bike and maneuvering it in the angle that I wanted it to be in.
