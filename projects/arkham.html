arkham knight poster

Confession: the Arkham Knight is a character from a Batman video game that I have not completed. I created a gradient mesh of him because he looks really cool (cooler than Batman!) and I thought that all the aspects of his costume would be a fun challenge for a gradient mesh in Illustrator. I ended up having a great time creating this as it taught me a lot about using the gradient mesh tool as well as how lighting works on different types of surfaces. I decided to put it on a movie poster that would be a side story within the Batman universe.
