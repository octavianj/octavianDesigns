culture cross

This was a personal project created as an exercise to meld the African and Japanese cultures. It consists of an African Geisha wearing a kimono with African dashiki designs surrounded by a dragon (a popular symbol in Asian culture), a lion (a famous symbol in African culture), and a few flowers from both of these locations. The lion represents royalty, strength, conquest, valor, pride, wisdom, authority, courage and protection. The dragon symbolizes supernatural power, wisdom, strength and hidden knowledge. These elements are all encompassed in a color palette that also has meanings relevant to the concept. The blue/green represents peace, togetherness, love and harmony. Purple represents royalty and prosperity. Red represents bloodshed by Africans who died in their fight for liberation and the shared blood of the African people. Gold represents wealth and nobility.
